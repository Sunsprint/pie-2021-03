About Us

Get to know us, the team.

Jonas Kazlauskas

Big gramps energy, likes plants and sunlight. He occasionally wears green and likes digging holes in his free time.

Jonas wrote a few lines of code and plugged and unplugged the arduino for this project. He hopes to one day replace the cup noodles with baked beans.

Kei Chua

Kei likes to play piano and sew clothes. In her free time, you can find her eating $1 oysters and giggling.

Kei was the visionary and sole mechanical engineer for the cup noodle machineTM. She worked on scrounging for free stuff and designed/built the mechanical components of the machine.

Shamama Sirroon

Shamama likes to spend her free time reading, eating ramen, and playing Nintendo games. She loves working on her Formula team and doing robotics research as well. Shamama is always down for an adventure and loves roller coasters, dogs, and rats.

During this project, Shamama helped out with mechanical fabrication and design, as well as helping out with the project deliverable website.

Tolulope Oshinowo

From software engineering to music production, Tolu likes to dabble in a variety of activities. That mentality was no different during his time spent on this project, as he would bounce around from helping out with firmware and setting up the ordering web app, to being a shop buddy.

In his free time Tolu enjoys creating digital art, learning about blockchain technology, and of course, making music.

Walter Villa

Walter enjoys photography as well as creating YouTube videos. He enjoys time in the gym, whether that be boxing, weight lifting, or playing volleyball. Walter also works as a Lead UX Designer at Road to Uni - a non profit organization aimed at democratizing the college application process, making it easier for students to successfully apply to college.

For this project, Walter worked on learning and getting experience in front-end software development, practicing writing in Javascript and working with the ReactJS framework. Additionally, he focused on helping gather the materials for the final website presentation.
